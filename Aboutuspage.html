About us best high
quality catering in Edinburgh

Dignans is a high quality outside catering company based in Edinburgh. They have been established for five years and work from a café/brassiere which is the other side of their business. Currently they have regular customers and several contracts for catering. These are from local businesses and offices in the immediate area surrounding the café, from where the catering is based. In addition they get one off clients requiring services like celebration buffets.
